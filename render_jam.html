IMAGE DESCRIPTION:

A tree in a starry night

DESCRIPTION OF HOW THIS IMAGE WAS CREATED:

I have used fractals to create the tree. The idea is similar to what we have done in one of the homeworks. I have used a recursive approach to create the branches of the tree. On every iteration, the length of the new branch is decremented by a factor of 1.3. I have used functions of trigonometry to calculate the two end points of every new branch that is to be added.

Details of Calculations:

I started with a vertical line (x1,y1) to (x2,y2). Initially x1 and x2 are 0 and y1 and y2 are -1 and 0.4(length of trunk of the tree) respectively. On the next iteration, the end point(x2,y2) of the tree trunk becomes the starting point (x1,y1) of the next two branches. The end points of these branches are calculated by the formula:

x2 = x1 + length * sin(angle)

y2 = y1 + length * cos(angle)

This goes on recursively untill limit is reached. For this render, I have set recursion depth to be 13
